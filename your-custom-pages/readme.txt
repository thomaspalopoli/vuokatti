Make Frontend Template - Custom Page Builder


HOW TO CREATE MY CUSTOM PAGE?

1) Open builder/index.html file in your browser.

2) Create your pages with Page Builder: select your elements, change text and color.

3) Once you have finished, export your pages by clicking on Export button in bottom right sidebar.

4) A zip file of your custom pages will be uploaded here!
