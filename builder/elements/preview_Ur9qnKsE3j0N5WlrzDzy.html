Want to know more about our Family ?

We are a family of three - Thomas, Kristina and Elio. During the past three years, we have lived in Bordeaux (France), Saint-Petersburg (Russia), Silvi (Italy) and Tallinn (Estonia). Thomas is educated as Mechanical Engineer and work now as a Creative Digital Entrepreneur. Kristina is educated in Public Relations and Advertising and she recently got a Bachelor in Hospitality Management. She now is a wonderful Mom and spend time developing Creative Projects as an Entrepreneur. Elio arrived on this planet in Summer 2020 and his biggest mission now is Exploration of the wonders of human life.

We moved a lot around Europe and found out that one thing is missing to all of these travels: COMMUNITY.

Travel brings power and love back into your life. - Rumi

That is why we created this project and wanted to share it with the world, and you. We want to meet new like-minded people. We want to create a life of exploration and connection, growing together on the way. Come and join us. Let's make this life incredible and exciting together !
