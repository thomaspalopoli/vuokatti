Pilot Project : Vuokatti, in the Heart of Finland

The pilot project is the first step in building this community of Semi-nomadic families. We are looking for 3 families who will join us and co-create this adventure together.

Finland is known as the land of a thousand lakes. Its nature is very varied and rich. There are forests, mountains, rivers. In winter you can ski and snowboard, ice diving or go to have a ride with the dog flock. It the summer you can go mountain biking, hiking, fishing, swimming. You can really find your place in all four seasons there.

Vuokatti is a holiday village located on the hills next to the slopes. It is calm and relaxing. There are different activities around such as swimming pool, gym, kids activity park, aqua-park, bowling etc. It’s in 10 minutes ride from little town Sotkamo with shops and restaurants. 2 hours away from the international airport of Ouly and 6 hours away from the capital of Finland- Helsinki. If you go even more north from Vuokatti you can see the Northern lights in Lapland and visit the North Polar Circle.

50meters away from the slopes are located our 2 Finnish chalets - each divided in 2 houses with independent entrances. There is a big free area that we are planning to make as a little garden in the summer.
